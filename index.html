About Me

About Me

I am a curious and versatile PM with a background in both business and UX. Throughout my career, I have been deeply interested in the field of UX. As I have taken on various UX responsibilities, my passion for UX research has grown stronger. I am eager to make a transition from PM to UXR to pursue this interest further and continue to develop my skills in this area.

I believe
  • Better solutions come from diverse perspectives. I am always eager to collaborate with others.
  • Good designs come from good research. As someone with a deep passion for empathizing with users, I am committed to understanding their needs, behaviors, and motivations to inform my design decisions.
I'm always looking for opportunities to learn and develop new skills, and I believe that continuous learning is the key to success in any field. I'm excited to bring my unique combination of skills and experience to any project or organization.
